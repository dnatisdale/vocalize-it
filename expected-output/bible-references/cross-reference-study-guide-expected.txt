This Bible study explores the topic of faith and perseverance.

Our primary reading is the Book of James, chapter one, verses two through four.

We will also look at supporting scriptures, including the Book of Romans, chapter five, verses three through five, the Book of Hebrews, chapter twelve, verses one and two, First Peter, chapter one, verses six and seven, and Philippians, chapter four, verses six and seven.

As you reflect on these passages, consider how perseverance strengthens faith, and what these scriptures teach us about walking through suffering.
