Our primary passage is the Book of Romans, chapter twelve, verses one and two.

We have several additional references to support this. They are the Book of Genesis, chapter one, verse twenty-seven, the Book of Psalms, chapter one hundred nineteen, verse one hundred five, Isaiah, chapter forty, verse thirty-one, the Gospel of John, chapter fourteen, verse six, the Book of Acts, chapter one, verse eight, Ephesians, chapter two, verses eight through ten, Colossians, chapter three, verses twelve through seventeen, and First Peter, chapter five, verse seven.

Take time to study each passage and reflect on how they relate to spiritual transformation.
